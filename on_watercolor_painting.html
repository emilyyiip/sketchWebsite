On Watercolor Painting

November 2, 2025

this summer, I’ve been painting more with watercolors. i just missed getting lost in the process of creating art. in my lifetime, I’ve dabbled in more mediums than I can count with my fingers. however, I haven’t touched watercolors since my art class in third grade. yet, my hands still remembered a few of the techniques. watercolor is so simple. no extensive setup, no fancy supplies, easily portable. the same effects created with Holbein watercolors can be done with Crayola student grade paint pans. it’s the pure skill of the artist. the aesthetic of the paintings is just so beautiful. the delicate translucence, the brushwork, how each layer is so carefully thought out and executed to form the bigger picture. I’m grateful that watercolor has gotten me back into painting.
