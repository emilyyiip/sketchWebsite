Hi, I'm Emily.

I am from San Francisco, California, and I am currently studying data science, environmental sociology, and design at the University of California San Diego. I am interested in geospatial data science and am currently doing research at the Scripps Institution of Oceanography.

On campus, I support students through crisis response and building communities as a resident assistant. Previously, I have also organized events on behalf of Claude by Anthropic as a campus ambassador.

I previously interned at NASA where I did data analysis for plane operations, at the World Wildlife Fund researching AI models for wildlife detection in airports, at the United States Space Force doing data analysis of ground operations.

I am also an artist of all sorts– my favorite mediums are watercolor and oil pastel, but I also make stained glass and ceramics. I also really like dogs. Feel free to connect!
